About MoveFit Physiotherapy Center

At MoveFit, we are dedicated to providing personalized physiotherapy care to help you recover from injury, manage pain, and improve your overall physical well-being. Our team of certified physiotherapists is committed to your health journey.

Dr. Shivendra Vikram Singh

Bachlor of Physiotherapy(BPT), MPT (Orthopedics)

Dr. Shivendra Vikram Singh is trained in orthopedic (PSRI Hospital, New Delhi and ESIC Hospital, Faridabad) and sports physiotherapy (Safderganj Sports Injury Center, New Delhi). He has a 2 year of experience working as a Senior Physiotherapist at Neuromotion Physiotherapy Clinic, Faridabad. He is certified Cup Therapist and Needling and Taping Practitioner. He is passionate about helping patients regain their mobility and live a pain-free life through evidence-based practices and compassionate care.
